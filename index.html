The Heart of the matter


It’s important to look after your heart – but it can be difficult to determine if it’s functioning well. Your blood pressure, cholesterol levels, BMI, level of physical activity and diet are all key factors in cardiovascular health. The good news is these factors are easily checked and monitored via a health kiosk or health assessment.

From the results of the BUPA health kiosks used at our offices last year, Accenture male employees showed more health risks than female employees. Its Men’s Health Week from 13-19th June, so why not make a commitment to take stock of your health this month?

This postcard details the options available to you to assess your health and advice to help you stay on top of your health goals following an assessment.
